Goswami Ganesh Dutta Sanatan Dharma College (GGDSDC) is a college of Panjab University located in the North Indian city of Chandigarh.[1] Established in 1973, the GGDSD college is spread over 16.5 acres of land. The college has been awarded A+ grade by the National Assessment and Accreditation Council (NAAC), Bangalore.[2] The college is ranked 70th among colleges in India by the National Institutional Ranking Framework (NIRF), MHRD in 2024.[3] The college is also recognized as 'College with Potential for Excellence' by the University Grants Commission (UGC), Delhi.[4] Programmes GGDSDC offers education in the following courses: Postgraduate degree courses M.Sc.
